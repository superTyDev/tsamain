About Us

Welcome to our annual prism concert here at George Jenkins High School! Just like a prism reflects the full range of light, Prism Performances relects a variety of skills and acts.

Since 2005, when this tradition first started, we had an annual concert with an explosion of events, from music shows to culinary miracles. This year's focus is on the US's variety of culture. With a variety of exhibits - including appearences from exceptional students at George Jenkins - enjoy Florida's diversity!

Recently, the pandemic pushed Prism Performances online. But, this didn't stop us from offering unique virtual events!
